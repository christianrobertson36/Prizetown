# Legal notes before public launch

Prizetown is only a technical starter app. Before taking payments or opening competitions publicly, get proper UK legal advice.

Important launch checks:

- Competition model: skill competition, free draw, or another lawful model.
- Clear free entry route if used.
- Clear terms and conditions.
- Privacy policy and cookie policy.
- Age restrictions and eligibility rules.
- Payment provider terms for competition/raffle-style businesses.
- Fair draw process and audit logs.

Do not treat this starter as legal compliance by itself.
